what

Abortion is when a pregnancy is ended so that it doesn't result in the birth of a child. Sometimes it is called 'termination of pregnancy'.

abortion process types

  • Medical abortion

    • Medical abortion involves taking two sets of tablets to end a pregnancy. It's available before 9 weeks of pregnancy.

  • Surgical abortion

    • Surgical abortion involves a local or general anaesthetic and/or sedation, and a minor operation to remove the pregnancy tissue.
